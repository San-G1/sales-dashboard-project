🟦 Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

🎯 Objective
I built this dashboard to understand how sales are performing over time, which areas contribute the most revenue, and how delivery efficiency impacts overall business performance.

📊 About the Dataset
This project uses the Superstore Sales dataset, which includes order details, customer information, product categories, regions, and shipping data.

🛠️ Tools Used
• Power BI
• DAX
• Data Modeling

🧱 What I Did
• Cleaned and prepared the dataset for analysis
• Created a Date Table to properly analyze trends over time
• Built relationships between tables for accurate filtering
• Added a custom column:
👉 Delivery Duration (Ship Date – Order Date)
• Created key measures like:
 - Total Sales
 - Total Orders
 - Sales per day
 - Orders per cstomer
 - Average Sales per Order
 - Average Delivery Duration

📈 Dashboard Highlights
• A monthly sales trend to track performance across the year
• KPI cards giving a quick overview of business performance
• Sales breakdown by category and region
• Delivery performance by shipping mode
• A scatter plot showing the relationship between delivery time and sales
• Interactive filters for deeper exploration

🧠 Key Insights
• Sales peak towards the end of the year, especially around November
• The Technology category contributes the highest revenue
• The West region performs better compared to others
• Standard Class shipping has the longest delivery duration
• Faster delivery does not always mean higher sales — other factors also play a role
• Regions with slightly higher delivery times can still generate strong sales

📌 Conclusion
This dashboard provides a clear view of both sales performance and operational efficiency. It helps identify where the business is doing well and where improvements can be made, especially in delivery and regional performance.
